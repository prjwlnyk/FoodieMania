What are we?

Foodie Mania in Bengaluru serves different foods for each season, creating dishes for whatever is fresh on the market. With a wealth of resources from farmers, fishermen and artisans, the kitchen sources pristine ingredients for each of its dishes. Foodie mania is loved by people from all across India and a go-to place for the international tourists. “Flavors shine as tradition melds with innovation.” Sound yummy? You bet!
